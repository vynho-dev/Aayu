01 · Foundation

Five principles that drive every screen

Aayu is used at the worst moment of a family's life — a hospitalization and a denied claim. Every decision serves clarity and confidence, never delight for its own sake.

Hope before mechanics
Lead with the result — money recoverable — then the detail.
Trust over decoration
Visible encryption, "pay only if we win," human review. No dark patterns.
Clarity over noise
One primary action per screen. Generous whitespace. No gradients.
Calm, not clinical-cold
Warm neutrals and one trustworthy teal — human, not sterile.
Effortless on a cheap phone
Mobile-first, low-end Android, minimal typing, large tap targets.

09 · Building blocks

Components, CTAs & patterns

Every screen above is composed from this small, reusable set. Reference the live Components cards in the Design System tab for full states.

Buttons & CTAs
One primary (teal) per screen. Secondary = hairline border. Ghost = text only. 44px tall. Press scales to 0.98.
Cards
White surface, 0.5px hairline border, 12px radius, no shadow. Colored tint only for positive/attention emphasis.
Spacing & hierarchy
8px base. 16px gutters. Section rhythm in 24/32/48. Display size reserved for the outcome number.
Status & trust
Chips pair color + icon + text. Trust badges persist on claim and result. Amber = review, never alarm.

11 · Inspiration

Warmth + evidence

Aayu's signature sits at the intersection of four references — inform, don't copy. The blend is deliberate: fintech calm keeps it trustworthy, health softness keeps it human, challenger honesty keeps it plain, public-service clarity keeps it usable on any phone.

Premium fintech calm
Restraint, whitespace, confidence with money. Flat surfaces, hairline borders.
Modern health apps
Reassuring, soft, warm neutrals — a hospital that feels human, not sterile.
Insurance challengers
Plain language, honesty, "on your side" — never corporate legalese.
Public-service clarity
High contrast, big targets, works on low-end Android at 200% text scale.
