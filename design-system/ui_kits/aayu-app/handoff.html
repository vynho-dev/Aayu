04 · Redlines

Spacing & sizing

All spacing is on the 8px base. A standard card: 16px padding, 12px radius, 0.5px hairline border, no shadow. Controls are 44px tall.

↕ 16 pad 16
Card title
Body copy sits 6px below the title. The card border is 0.5px; radius 12px.
r:12
Tap targets — min 44×44px, always.
Side padding — 16px on mobile, content max 720px on desktop.
Section rhythm — 24 / 32 / 48px between blocks.
Internal gaps — 8 / 12 / 16px within components.
Focus ring — 0 0 0 3px rgba(15,110,86,.25).
